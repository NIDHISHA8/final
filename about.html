CAFETERIA

University has a well established 
Cafeteria located on the campus providing
 wide range of foods including north & 
South Indian as well as fast food for
 the benefit of the students and staff.   
       Food offered by the Cafeteria is
 hygienic and it offers a taste of home,
 with wholesome freshly-cooked dishes 
from around the country. 
       With its friendly atmosphere, 
the cafeteria serves traditional, 
south and north Indian dishes, 
all cooked in the in-house kitchens. 
       Food is nutritious and with 
tastes to suit the needs of the diverse
 student community studying in the university.
       A new modern cafeteria is coming up with
 250 seating capacity measuring about 
9000 sq ft area just below the state of
 the art Auditorium in the campus
